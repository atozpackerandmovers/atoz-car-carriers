FAQ

Questions customers ask before booking car transport

How long does car transport delivery take?

Delivery time depends on pickup city, delivery city, route availability, carrier type and road conditions. Odisha nearby routes can be faster, while long-haul metro routes can take several days.

Is transit insurance available?

Transit insurance assistance is available. Final insurance terms, coverage, exclusions and claim documents are explained before booking confirmation.

How is payment handled?

The team confirms route, carrier type and service requirement before payment details are shared. GST invoice support can be provided where applicable.

Can I track my vehicle?

You can request status updates by phone or WhatsApp using your enquiry ID or booking details.

What documents are required?

Usually RC details, customer ID/contact details and pickup handover confirmation are required. The team may request additional details depending on route and vehicle type.

What happens if there is damage?

Vehicle condition photos and inspection notes are important. Any claim process depends on insurance terms, inspection records and carrier handover documentation.
